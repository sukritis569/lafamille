My Beautiful Family

This is my little diary of love for Mumma, Mausi, Mausa Ji, Bhaiya, and Papa.

Mumma

You are my strength, my peace, and my eternal warmth. Your love surrounds me even when you're silent. I’m grateful for your sacrifices and hugs that fix everything.

Mausi

You’ve always treated me like your own. Your gentle heart, your laughter, and your endless care are blessings I cherish every day. You are my second mumma in every way.

Mausa Ji

Your calmness and guidance have been my moral compass. Thank you for your silent strength, for believing in me, and for always showing up when I need it most.

Bhaiya

You’re not just my brother, you're my best friend, my secret keeper, and my forever supporter. Life feels easier with you around, even from a distance.

Papa

Your silent love, your lessons, your protective presence—it's everything. Thank you for being my superhero in a simple shirt. I’ll always be your little girl.
